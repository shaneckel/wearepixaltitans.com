Pixel Titans are a small team of people brought together by a love of making the games we want to play… We know that sounds like every other development team but we’re special. Shut up.

Pixel Titans was founded by video director Thom Glunt and programmer Stephen Raney. The two of them had worked together for years on various music videos and commercials until they realized they were the perfect partners to make games. STRAFE® started as a passion project between 2 people and has grown to a team of 10 in full time development. Pixel Titans aims to glorify older game design while modernizing it and making the Benjamin Button of games. We make tough games for tough people.
